Design §2 — Cars, physics & combat

Shared vehicle core. Player and AI only send intent.

Car (CharacterBody2D)

  • Physics: accelerate / brake-reverse / rotate while moving; linear friction when coasting
  • Exports: max_speed, acceleration, reverse_speed, turn_speed, friction, max_health, fire_cooldown, missile_damage, wall_slowdown_factor
  • API: set_throttle(), set_steer(), try_fire() — controllers call these
  • Wall hit: collision with StaticBody2D walls → multiply velocity (slowdown), no HP loss in v1
  • HP bar: ProgressBar (or ColorRect) above car, billboard-style (no rotation with car, or slight offset)
  • Death: HP ≤ 0 → CPUParticles2D burst + queue_free (or hide + disable) → emit died

Controllers

PlayerCar
WASD / Arrows → throttle & steer
Space → try_fire()
Camera2D as child of player (or Main follows player)
AICar
Follow track Path2D waypoints
Steer toward look-ahead point
If target in range + LOS cone → try_fire()

Missile (Area2D)

  • Spawns at car nose, constant velocity in facing direction
  • Damage: default 15 (export 10–20 range)
  • On body/area enter car → apply damage, free self; free on wall hit or lifetime timeout
  • Ignore owner (no self-hit) for a short grace or by reference

Signals (clean seams → option 3 later)

health_changed(current, max) · died · fired
